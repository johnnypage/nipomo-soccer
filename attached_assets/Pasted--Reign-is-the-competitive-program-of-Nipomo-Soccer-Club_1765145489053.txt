
Reign is the competitive program of Nipomo Soccer Club. It exists to give skilled and committed young athletes in Nipomo a complete pathway to pursue advanced soccer without leaving town or taking on high financial burdens. We believe that players should be able to reach their potential in their own community, surrounded by teammates and families who share their pride in where they are from.

Reign delivers structured year round development that focuses on strong fundamentals, tactical understanding, mental toughness, and character on and off the field. Players train with consistency, challenge themselves in meaningful competition, and learn how to lead within a true team culture.

Reign is designed around three key principles.
	1.	Quality Coaching
Our staff includes licensed and experienced coaches who volunteer their time to develop local talent. We focus on teaching the game the right way. Every training session is planned with purpose. Every player receives coaching attention that helps them grow.
	2.	Affordability
Traditional club soccer often costs families thousands of dollars per year. Our goal is to keep club participation under $300 per year through sponsorships, donations, and fundraising. We are committed to making Reign accessible for families who believe in the work and want their child to compete.
	3.	Earned Opportunity
Players are selected based on ability, attitude, and effort. They earn their spot through performance in Roots and Rise or through open tryouts. Talent matters, but character and commitment matter just as much. Families should never be priced out of development.

Reign offers competitive teams beginning at U8 and continuing through high school age groups. Our current active teams include Girls U8, U9, U10, and U11, and Boys U8, U10, and two U14 teams. Additional teams are forming as more players commit to the program.

Training includes multiple weekly practices, preseason preparation, and ongoing development throughout the year. Teams compete in league play and tournaments to give players valuable game experience and increasingly challenging competition.

We regularly offer open evaluations for new players. Interested families can join the interest list to receive updates about team openings, tryout dates, and program news.

Reign is the top of the player pathway at Nipomo Soccer Club. Players begin in Roots for foundational learning, progress into Rise for skill advancement and confidence building, and reach Reign when they are ready for the extended commitment and competition of a year round environment. It is a clear pathway from recreational fun to advanced play, all within Nipomo.

Most importantly, Reign reflects what youth sports should be. It is about community. It is about growth. It is about helping local kids discover how much they can accomplish when they are surrounded by support, challenge, and pride in the work they put in.

Reign players represent Nipomo. They work hard for their teammates. They show up with purpose. They compete with heart. And they help strengthen our town one season at a time.
